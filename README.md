# PageTurner
A full-stack web application to track your reading progress, log reading sessions, and build consistent reading habits with detailed statistics and goal tracking.
üìö Features

User Authentication: Secure registration and login system with JWT tokens
Book Management: Add, edit, delete, and organize your reading library
Reading Sessions: Log your daily reading progress with notes
Progress Tracking: Visual progress bars and completion percentages for each book
Reading Statistics:

Average pages per day (7-day and 30-day)
Active reading days tracking
Total books and completion count


Monthly Goals: Set and track monthly reading goals for books and pages
Star Ratings: Rate your books from 1-5 stars
Reading Streaks: Track your reading consistency with streak counters
Responsive Design: Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
